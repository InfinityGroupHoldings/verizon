Wait a sec... What is mmWave?

Our goal is to provide the most accurate map of 5G mmWave in the US for all 3 major carriers. This is the Verizon Wireless subdomain of mmWave.one. Our development team is hard at work cooresponding with Verizon in hopes to start a partnership, in the mean time we are relying on user feedback. Our developers are Esmél and PlanMan1717. If you would like to assist with the project, please email Esmél. Our map can be found here, thanks. You can also report mmWave coverage on the Verizon network through this form.
